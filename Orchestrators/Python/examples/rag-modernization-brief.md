# RAG Modernization Brief

We need to design a retrieval-augmented generation platform for internal support engineers and customer success teams.

## Business outcome

- reduce mean time to resolution for support escalations
- improve answer consistency across teams
- lower the number of manual knowledge lookups

## Corpus

- product documentation
- knowledge base articles
- support tickets
- runbooks
- change logs

## Constraints

- sub-3 second median response time
- multi-tenant access controls
- auditable citations
- budget sensitivity at scale
- strong preference for phased rollout and measurable evaluation gates
